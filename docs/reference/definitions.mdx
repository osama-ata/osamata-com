# Definitions

Let's discover the common definitions in **Construction Contracts**.

:::info Definitions of Contract

<Tabs>

  <TabItem value="EG" label="Egypt">
  `"A contract is an agreement between two or more parties that creates, modifies, or extinguishes obligations."` 
  - Article 89 of the Egyptian Civil Code
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem value="SA" label="KSA">
  
  `"A contract is concluded by the concurrence of an offer and acceptance to create a legal effect, 
  subject to the legal provisions governing the conclusion of contracts."`
  - Article 31 of CTL "Civil Transactions Law" in KSA
  </TabItem>

    <TabItem value="UK" label="UK">
  A contract is a legally binding promise (written or oral) by one party to fulfil an obligation to another party in return for consideration. A basic binding contract must comprise four key elements: 
  - offer, 
  - acceptance, 
  - consideration, and 
  - intent to create legal relations.
  </TabItem>


</Tabs>
:::

These Definitions introduce the concept of **Contracts**,  the **essential elements** that make a contract legally binding, and also introduce further topics such as **Offer, Acceptance, Consideration, and Intent**.
